Media Rating Application

Software,Libraries, and Frameworks: JavaScript, MongoDB, Handlebars, Express (Node.js).

Mastersite is a media rating website that utilizes a MongoDB NoSQL database of more than 20,000 pieces of media, allowing you to search, view, and rate the media. Not shown, but also included, is admin capabilities, allowing admin users to edit, add, and delete media. Admin users can also delete and mute regular user accounts.
